2% Salicylic Acid Anti-Acne Face Serum: An intensely exfoliating beta hydroxy acid, Salicylic Acid is used to control acne breakouts and excess sebum production. The oil-soluble properties of this face serum let it penetrate deep into the skin and remove dead cells, giving you clear and healthy skin. The combination of natural ingredients like Green Tea, Aloe Vera and Willow Bark prevents the purging and appearance of whiteheads and blackheads and eventually stops acne formation. As an added benefit, a Hyaluronic Acid base adds hydration and moisturisation, leaving you with velvety soft skin. Free from sulphates, parabens, dyes, fragrances, essential oils, and silicones, this face serum is safe to use and causes no harm.

Step 1: Apply 2-3 drops on cleansed face at night. Step 2: Spread the serum evenly using your fingertips and let it absorb completely. Step 3: Start by using it every alternate day for two weeks, and post that use it every day. Quick Tip: Use a hydrating moisturiser after using this serum.
